Reynaldo Armenta Bravo | WDD 130

My name is Reynaldo but everyone calls me Rey. I was born in Mexico City but raised in Colonia Juarez, a small town in the state of Chihuahua. I really like the outdoors, camping, hiking, biking, swiming, exploring and ocassionally fishing. I also like to play sports, mainly volleyball, basketball and tennis, though I'll pretty much try anything. I served my mission in Brazil, the Curitiba Sul mission.

Chihuahua, Mexico

Chihuahua is the biggest state in Mexico located in northern Mexico. It is surrounded by the mexican states of Sonora, Sinaloa, Durango and Coahuila and the US states of Texas and New Mexico. It is a state that has a lot of dessert but also a lot of mountains. It is filled with culture and history.
