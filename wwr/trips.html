Trips

Koda's Fjord

Perfect for scenic views. You'll love and enjoy this location. It is quite fast and challenging so you'll have to be quick and careful if you want to take any pictures home.

Rock River

Bet ready to hit rock bottom. It'll be challenging and you'll have to work well with your group to be able to avoid the big rocks. You'll have a blast.

Nayla River

Get ready to get shocked, there are plenty of action wetting sections. You'll have to direct the raft with difficulty since most of the time you'll be going up and down, with little time to plan.
